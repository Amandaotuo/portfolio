    • 5. Project Description: Book Cover

    The "A Quiet Place" book cover design project involves the creation of a captivating and evocative cover for a literary work titled "A Quiet Place." The book cover was designed using Adobe Photoshop and size of 8.5 inches by 11 inches. The goal is to craft a visually compelling cover that captures the essence of the story and entices readers to explore its contents
